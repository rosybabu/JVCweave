Where home is a haven. And artistry unfolds.

Sanctuary of serenity. Center of comfort. Cross the threshold and feel—a sense of arrival, of relief. Let it wash over you as you immerse yourself in artisanal living, delicately crafted for a life of intention. In each of our residences, neutral tones and hand-selected finishes round out space for shared moments and memories worth making, while beautiful views of JVC from the balcony envelop and excite.

Design Features
Breathable layouts

Composed for seamless living, each of our 2- and 3-bedroom residences provide ample space to move freely alongside balanced function and form.

Welcoming kitchens

Fully-fitted kitchens with luxurious stone splash back and countertops welcome mealtime memories.

Calming features

Elements of bronze and beige tones with hints of rose gold tie back to the landscape and make for a peaceful home.

Intentional curation

Hand-selected pieces create a sense of harmony, while leaving room for personal curation. 

location
Piece of community. Place of calm.

Resting in the tranquil ambiance of the Jumeirah Village Circle, The Weave tucks you into a gated family-friendly neighborhood that remains closely connected to the vibrancy of downtown Dubai and the attractions of the beachfront. A short drive to the Dubai Al Maktoum Intl (DWC) Airport and ideal placement near destinations including the Marina, beautiful beaches, golf courses, Miracle Garden and Mall of the Emirates, living here makes going to and from experiences effortless.
